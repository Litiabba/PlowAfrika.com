About PlowAfrika

PlowAfrika is transforming African agriculture through technology, innovation, and inclusive systems that empower smallholder farmers to thrive. We deliver end-to-end agricultural solutions; from digital and AI-powered advisory to input credit and market access; designed to increase productivity, strengthen resilience, and unlock the business potential of every farmer.

Vision & Mission

Our Mission

To empower Africa’s smallholder farmers with knowledge, finance, and market access, enabling sustainable livelihoods and resilient food systems.

Our Vision

A future where African agriculture works for everyone; smart, inclusive, profitable, and climate-resilient.

Our Story

PlowAfrika exists to bridge the gaps holding African farmers back. We believe technology alone is not enough, farmers also need trust, human support, local knowledge, and access to finance and markets.
By combining AI-driven advisory tools, on-ground agent networks, and inclusive financial models, we support farmers throughout the entire farming cycle, whether they use smartphones or not.
Through initiatives like FarmWiser, AGRI IMPACT, and Grow2Thrive, we deliver integrated solutions that help farmers move from subsistence to sustainability, and from survival to prosperity.
Because when farmers are fully supported, they won’t only grow crops they'll build resilient livelihoods and a stronger food system for Africa.

Founder's Quote

“I grew up around farming communities and saw firsthand the challenges farmers faced; fragmented services, lack of accountability, and little real impact on their livelihoods. Farmers were expected to succeed without access to credit, without advisory support, and without the full set of services needed to run farming as a business.”

Femi Tobe, Co-Founder, PlowAfrika

Our Core Beliefs

At PlowAfrika, our work is guided by principles that put farmers first and drive sustainable agricultural impact across Africa.

Farmer-Centric

Everything we do starts with the needs, realities, and voices of smallholder farmers—ensuring solutions are accessible, practical, and truly empowering.

Sustainability & Resilience

We prioritize climate-smart practices, soil health, and long-term viability to build farming systems that withstand shocks and thrive for generations.

Inclusivity & Equity

No farmer left behind—regardless of gender, location, literacy, or smartphone access—we design inclusive models that bridge systemic gaps.

Innovation with Purpose

Technology and data serve farmers, not the other way around—combining AI, local knowledge, and human support for real, measurable impact.

Integrity & Transparency

We build trust through accountability, fair partnerships, and open communication—delivering on promises to farmers, partners, and communities.

Prosperity for All

Our goal is thriving livelihoods—turning subsistence farming into profitable, dignified businesses that strengthen families and food systems.
